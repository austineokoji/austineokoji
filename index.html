Maintenance Systems Downtime Reduction Initiative

RoleMechanical Engineering Technician
TimeframeMar 2015 — Jul 2018
DisciplineMaintenance · Diagnostics

Overview

A structured, technician-led effort to improve equipment uptime through disciplined inventory management, calibration monitoring, and root-cause diagnostics — resulting in a measured 30% reduction in downtime.

Approach

  1. 01 Manage spare-parts inventory to guarantee availability for emergency repairs
  2. 02 Monitor tools and equipment calibration on a routine schedule
  3. 03 Diagnose mechanical problems and design corrective solutions
  4. 04 Execute routine maintenance against blueprint and 3D plan specifications
  5. 05 Log all maintenance activity into a comprehensive reference system

Focus Areas

Preventive maintenance scheduling, spare-parts logistics, precise blueprint-driven repair work, and maintenance documentation built for long-term reference.

Result

Consistent, calibrated equipment and a documented maintenance history directly contributed to a measurable drop in unplanned downtime.
